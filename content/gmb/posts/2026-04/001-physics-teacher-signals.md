## Caption

The IIT tag is a weak signal for picking a Physics teacher. Here is what matters more.

In a real 30-minute class, watch three things. One, does the teacher start from the simplest version of the problem or drop into the hard version to impress. The good ones start simple. Two, when a student says "I do not understand," does the teacher rephrase or repeat louder. The good ones rephrase. Three, how much gets written on the board versus projected on slides. Live Physics is dense board work, not slide decks.

Twenty years of teaching at Narayana, Bansal and Excel Physics, three signals I check every single time.
