## Strategic reads

1. **The "without coaching" cluster is a unique content opportunity.** No major coaching brand ranks here because it's counter-incentive for them. We can write "How to prepare for NEET from Class 11 without coaching — and the three moments when coaching actually moves the needle" — captures 1,000+/mo combined volume, KD range 10–17 (very achievable), and uses our honest-voice positioning as a differentiator.

2. **"Which coaching is best" queries** — these are big-volume (1.3K + 480 + 210 = 2K+/mo) but KD 24–44 means they're dominated by aggregator sites (Collegedunia, SelfStudys, PW). Our pillar can target these as secondary terms, not primary.

3. **"Which city is best for neet coaching" (50/mo)** — Jaipur-vs-Kota-vs-Sikar positioning piece. Strong contrarian angle fit: "Why Jaipur beats Kota for a lot of students (and the three cases where it doesn't)". Our founder worked at Bansal Kota, Narayana — credible authority.

4. **AI Overview optimization:** these exact question-format H2s should be used verbatim in the pillar piece and satellite articles. AI engines match user queries to exact H2 text.
